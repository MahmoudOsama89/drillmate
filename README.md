Privacy Policy — DrillMate
Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the DrillMate mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Your photos and projects stay on your device
When you create a chart, the App reads the photo you choose and converts it into a chart entirely on your device. Your photos, generated charts, and progress are stored only in the App's local storage. We do not upload, transmit, collect, or have access to your photos or projects.

2. No account, no personal data collection
The App has no sign-up and no user account. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, or location. The App contains no advertising and no third-party analytics SDKs.

3. Purchases
The optional one-time "Pro" unlock is processed by Google Play Billing. Google handles the transaction under its own policies; we do not receive or store your payment details. Your unlock is recorded on your device and restored through your Google account by Google Play.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
4. Permissions and internet
The App declares internet access only so Google Play Billing can verify and restore your purchase. It is not used to transmit your photos or projects. Choosing a photo uses the system photo picker, so the App does not request access to your full photo library.

5. Children
The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

6. Data retention and deletion
Delete a project inside the App, or uninstall the App, to remove its locally stored data. Purchase records are managed by Google Play.

7. Changes
We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date.
